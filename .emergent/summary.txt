<analysis>**original_problem_statement**: Build a secure, therapist-first web application for managing a therapy practice and supporting clinical decision-making.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS**
- **User Roles**: Therapist, Client, Super Admin, and Therapist Assistant.
- **Authentication**: Secure JWT-based login for all roles.
- **Practice Management**: Client profiles (editable), appointment scheduling (with availability, slot generation, and calendar blocking), session notes (SOAP & DAP), secure messaging, and payment tracking.
- **Clinical Support**: AI-powered assessment suggestion engine, therapy protocol builder, and homework/resource sharing.
- **Super Admin & Subscriptions**: A Super Admin role to manage therapists, clients, and a subscription module.
- **Therapist Assistant Role**: A restricted role to manage operational tasks without access to clinical data.
- **Clinical Workflow**: A mandatory, multi-step **Case History** must be completed by the therapist before any session notes can be created. Following this, a **Therapy Consent** form is generated, which the client must digitally sign before therapy can proceed.
- **Data Isolation & Security**: Therapists can only see their own assigned clients. Assistants are restricted. Clinical notes are not accessible to clients.
- **Global Standards**: The application must use India Standard Time (IST), DD/MM/YYYY date format, 24-hour (HH:mm) time format, and Indian Rupee (₹) currency.
- **UI/UX**: Profile photo support, search functionality, a comprehensive client profile view, and productivity features.

**User's preferred language**: English
