<analysis>**original_problem_statement**: Build a secure, therapist-first web application named **COGNISPACE** for practice management and clinical decision support.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS**
*   **Core Features**: Secure JWT-based authentication for Therapists, Clients, Super Admins, and Assistants. Includes client management, appointments, session notes, messaging, and payment tracking.
*   **TheraGenie & CogniVision**: An AI module for clinical intelligence, including the generation of editable, exportable (PDF) psychodiagnostic reports.
*   **Client Self-Registration**: Therapists must have a unique, permanent link they can share with clients to allow them to create their own profiles. A new public booking flow has also been requested where a new user can register while booking an appointment.
*   **Client-Facing Reports**: Clients must be able to view diagnostic reports shared by their therapist directly from their dashboard.
*   **In-App Notifications**: A role-aware, real-time notification system for various events, including morning briefings and daily payment summaries for therapists.
*   **Admin Features**: Admins must be able to manage therapists, including deleting their accounts. When a therapist is deleted, their clients should become orphaned and re-linkable to other therapists by an admin.
*   **Payment Reporting**: A comprehensive reporting dashboard for therapists to analyze revenue and track payments, filtered by month.
*   **Legal & Informational Pages**: The public-facing site must include pages for About, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, etc., with links in the footer.
*   **Profile Management**: Functionality for profile photo uploads.
*   **Manual Homework & Templates**: Therapists must be able to assign custom homework and also use/create templates.
*   **Public Booking Calendar**: A shareable public calendar link for each therapist, allowing new clients to register and book an appointment, which then requires therapist approval.
*   **Mobile-First Client UI**: The client-facing dashboard should be redesigned to function like a mobile app, with bottom navigation and simplified views.
*   **Shared Resources**: Therapists should be able to share resources (articles, PDFs) from the Resource Library with clients, who can then view them in their dashboard.
